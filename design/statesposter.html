In my Advanced Issues class during my senior year at Endicott College, I was given the task to create an engaging poster series to highlight data about two states assigned at random. We were given state database resources to analyze. Upon completion of my analysis, I decided to highlight the citizens of the states through their businesses, educational systems and ethnicities, while carefully focusing on how this information would be visually presented.
